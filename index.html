The Effects of Climate Change on U.S. Bee Population

Title for Project: "The Affects of Climate Change on U.S. Bee Population"

Purpose of Project: The main purpose of this project is to highlight how climate change has contributed to the decline of Bees throughout the U.S. over the last 30 years. Through this visualization, people can better view how staggering the decline has been, the causes of the decline, and how the decline affects the health of our planet.

Data Sources:

  • Bee Colony Population and Economic Data: 1980s-Present Day --- (United States Department of Agriculture)
  • Temperature Averages and other climate data by State: 1980s-Present Day --- (National Centers for Environmental Information)
  • Evidence of Bee decline being linked to changes in climate in the U.S. --- (American Association for the Advancement of Science)
  • Evidence of increased Bee decline from parasite linked to changes in temperature --- (National Center For Biotechnology Information)
